Lentils, but more wet

lentil soup--does it contain lentils? what kind of lentils does it contain? let's find out!

What you'll need:

  • my god it's full of lentils
  • chicken stock, chicken stock, chicken stock
  • salt
  • a mild onion
  • a carrot
  • some butter
  • spices

What you gotta do:

  1. Cut up the veggies
  2. Melt the butter and through the veg in, like, aggressively
  3. Toss in spices. It's about to get fragrant in here, y'all
  4. Cascade lentils into the good smelling stuff
  5. Stir 'em up
  6. Pour the stock now, y'all
  7. Heat the stock
  8. S.A.L.T.*
  9. Let them absorb stock
  10. Put some steamed rice in a bowl (you steamed the rice didn't you? keep up.)
  11. Slap on dollop of full fat greek yogurt
  12. Fairy dust it with some fresh parsley

*This is short for [Strategic Arms Limitation Treaty], which has nothing to do with food, but it is a juicy morsel of knowledge you may have only just learned (you're welcome). And no, it doesn't *actually* mean salt, this soup is quite bland.
