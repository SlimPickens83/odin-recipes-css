Lentils

It's just lentils.

What you'll need:

  • a whole mess of lentils
  • a fair amount, dare I say a lot, of water
  • salt

What you gotta do:

  1. Boil that water
  2. Get all of those lentils in there
  3. Toss salt in like it's going out of style
  4. Soak 'em
  5. Keep soaking
  6. Know they're done
  7. Drain those delish little mush kernels
  8. Plate 'em up and get out the ketchup
